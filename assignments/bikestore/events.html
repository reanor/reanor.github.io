Cycle the Continental Divide

Come and sample our Great Continental Bike Route fully supported and load free! We’ll travel from the lively resort town of Whitefish, located near the CDT, to the southern end of the spectacular Bannock Pass, one of Montana’s less-visited scenic gems. Not far away rests the town of Leodore, a notoriously difficult hitch on a remote dirt road.

  • Trip Dates: Jul 12 – Jul 18
  • Cost: $1,159.00
  • Location: Whitefish, MT
  • Physical Difficulty: Intermediate+
  • Route: CDT - Red Rock Pass - Idaho/Montana border
  • Requirements: Biking helmet, shin guards, gloves, pads, cross country performance bike

Early Birds

This program is created for youth aged 8-17 with low to medium mountain biking experience. Our groups will be formed based on your mountain biking experience as well as your age group and will be supported by our enthusiastic and knowledgeable guides. If necessary, we will provide the coaching on per group level.

Requirements

  • Equipment: biking helmet, shin guards (gloves and pads are optional but highly recommended)
  • Bike: cross country bike for entry level riders
Prices:
  • Camp Only: $287
  • Camp + Entry level Bike Rental: $412
  • Camp + Performance Bike Rental: $474

Bike and Camp

Allows groups of medium experienced bikers to engage in the cross terrain multiple-day adventure. Traverse multiple trails ranging from easily accessible cruise areas, to the slopy areas with daily breaks for camping at the most scenic locations on the trail.

Requirements

  • Equipment: biking helmet, shin guards
  • Bike: entry level cross country bike, or performance bike
Prices:
  • Camp Only: $287
  • Camp + Entry level Bike Rental: $412
  • Camp + Performance Bike Rental: $474

Downhill Biking

Take a lift up, bike down on one of the 14 downhill trails at Big Sky Resort. You can rent one of our bikes or bring your own. All you'll need is a bike haul lift ticket or bike haul season pass, available for purchase at our store.

Requirements

  • Equipment: biking helmet, shin guards, gloves, pads.
  • Bike: performance bike
Prices:
  • Camp Only: $287
  • Camp and Bike Haul Pass: $358
  • Camp + Bike Haul Pass + Entry level bike Rental: $462
  • Camp + Bike Haul Pass + Performance Bike Rental: $524
